MODULARIZED PACKAGE CARRIER

This innovation and entrepreneurship project that aims to design a modularized package carrier based on the DJI 600 Hexacopter that allows the users to easily switch between carrier boxes to meet different transportation needs.

DESIGN BACKGROUND

Logistics UAV is a popular topic right now and many companies have developed their own solutions such as Amazon and DHL. Logistics UAV has the advantages of timeliness and accurate positioning, which is useful in rural areas and for urgent deliveries. The project aims to design and develop actual functioning products that can be put into real-world use.

MODULARIZED CARRIER

The design is featured with modularization that allows the users to switch between different carrier boxes to meet different transportation needs. I designed the modularized structure by placing two sliding blocks on top of the carrier box and two sliding rails at the bottom of the loading platform.  

I also designed one of the modularized carrier box that targets small and urgent packages. It has a front door that allows the user to put in the package and a bottom door to drop the package at the destination.

FINAL RESULTS

This innovation and entrepreneurship project lasted over a year from 2020 to 2021. Apart from the mechanical subgroup where I worked in, the team also consists of other engineering majors. We collectively designed and construct different carrier boxes, along with a visual recognition system and a control software.   

The product won the first prize in Shanghai Mechanical Engineering Innovation Contest. Moreover, it was also put into test runs for package transportation on campus to facilitate studentsâ€™ lives.
